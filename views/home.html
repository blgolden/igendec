The iGenDec project is a collaboration with the University of Nebraska, Lincoln, Kansas State University, US Meat Animal Research Center, Nebraska and Theta Solutions, LLC. It is the result of a three year USDA funded project to develop a web-based beef cattle selection index tool that seed stock buyers can use to evaluate alternative purchase opportunities' impact on their marginal net returns.
